World-Class Amenities

Everything you need for the perfect stay

Conference Hall

Host meetings, seminars, and corporate events in our fully equipped conference hall featuring modern AV systems, LED lighting, and professional seating for up to 80 delegates.

Reception

Our welcoming 24/7 reception desk ensures every guest receives a seamless check-in experience. Our trained front-desk team is always on hand to assist with any request, day or night.

Restaurant

Savour expertly prepared Indian and Continental dishes in our elegantly designed restaurant. From a hearty breakfast buffet to a candlelit dinner, every meal is crafted to delight your palate.

Parking

Arrive with ease and peace of mind. Vaapi Comfort Inn offers ample, secure, and complimentary parking space within the property premises for all in-house guests.

Unwind in our beautifully maintained garden nestled among tall Himalayan pines and lush greenery. A perfect natural retreat for morning walks, outdoor seating, and refreshing fresh air.
